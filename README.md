# NFL_data_analysis
This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** analyzing NFL team performance from 2003 to 2023.   The dashboard focuses on offensive efficiency, defensive strength, and overall team balance using advanced data visualization techniques.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate **data modeling, analytical thinking, and storytelling with data**, rather than only visual creation.

---

## Objective
- Analyze offensive and defensive performance of NFL teams
- Identify balanced teams across different seasons
- Compare efficiency metrics such as passing performance and scoring
- Provide a clean, professional BI dashboard suitable for executive analysis

---

## Key Metrics (KPIs)
- **Average Points per Game**
- **Average Opponent Points per Game**
- **Passing Net Yards per Attempt**
- **Rushing Yards**
- **Offense vs Defense Balance**

---

## How to Use the Dashboard
- Use the **season range slicer** to analyze different periods and **team slicer** with the logos to analyze the different NFL teams
- Hover over visuals to view detailed tooltips
- Compare teams across offensive and defensive metrics

---

## Dashboard Visuals & Explanations

### 4️⃣ Team Performance KPIs (Cards)

This section presents high-level KPIs summarizing team performance over the selected seasons.

**Displayed KPIs:**
- **Average Points per Game**
- **Average Opponent Points per Game**
- **Average Passing Yards**
- **Average Rushing Yards**

**Insight:**  
These KPIs provide a quick performance snapshot, allowing instant evaluation of offensive style and defensive strength.

---

### Win/Loss Percentage by Season (Area Chart)

This area chart shows how a team’s **average win/loss percentage** evolves over time.

- **X-axis:** Season year  
- **Y-axis:** Average win/loss percentage  

**Insight:**  
The chart highlights performance trends, dominant periods, and rebuilding phases, offering historical context to team success.

---

### Average Win/Loss Percentage (Gauge Chart)

This gauge represents the **overall average win/loss percentage** across the selected seasons.

**Insight:**  
It condenses long-term performance into a single metric, making it ideal for quick comparisons and executive-level insights.

---

**Insight:** 
Together, these KPIs provide a high-level snapshot of how balanced a team is offensively and defensively, allowing for quick comparisons across teams and time periods.



### Average Points by Team (Treemap)
This treemap highlights the distribution of average points scored per game by each NFL team.

**Insight:**  
Teams with consistently high scoring averages occupy larger areas, making it easy to identify offensive dominance across seasons.

---

### Offense vs Defense Balance (Scatter Plot)
This scatter plot compares:
- **X-axis:** Average points scored per game  
- **Y-axis:** Average points conceded per game  

Quadrants help classify team profiles:
- **Elite Teams:** Strong offense and strong defense (2nd quadrant)  
- **Strong Offense:** High scoring but defensive weaknesses (3nd quadrant) 
- **Strong Defense:** Solid defense with lower offensive output (1nd quadrant) 
- **Below Average:** Underperformance in both metrics (4nd quadrant)  

**Insight:**  
Balanced teams tend to combine efficient scoring with controlled defensive performance, highlighting strategic consistency.

---

### Passing Efficiency (Bar Chart)
This bar chart ranks teams by **average net passing yards per attempt**.

**Insight:**  
Higher passing efficiency is strongly associated with teams appearing in the elite performance quadrant, reinforcing the importance of aerial offense in modern NFL play.

---

## Key Analytical Insights
- Teams with higher passing efficiency are more likely to maintain elite performance levels.
- Defensive performance shows lower variability compared to offensive output.
- Some teams compensate weaker defenses with exceptionally strong offensive production.
- Balanced teams tend to perform consistently across multiple seasons.
